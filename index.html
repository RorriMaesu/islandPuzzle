How to Play Puzzle Colony

Collect Resources

Click on tiles in the grid to collect resources:

  • W - Wood
  • S - Stone
  • F - Food

Build Structures

Use your resources to build structures on your island:

  • Hut - Costs 10 Wood
  • Farm - Costs 10 Food (generates Food every 10 seconds)
  • Mine - Costs 15 Stone (generates Stone every 10 seconds, unlocks at Level 2)

Level Up

Build 3 structures to reach Level 2 and unlock the Mine building.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Press R to reset the game.
